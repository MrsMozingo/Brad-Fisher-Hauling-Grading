Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 04/14/2024

Welcome to Brad Fisher Hauling and Grading. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our visitors and clients. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to ensure its protection.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us through our website forms, email communications, or phone calls. This may include:

  • Contact Information: Such as your name, email address, phone number, and any other contact details you provide.
  • Project Details: Information regarding the services you are interested in, such as hauling or grading requirements, to help us provide tailored services.
  • Feedback and Inquiries: Any information you provide as feedback or in inquiries.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used in the following ways:

  • To Provide Services: To manage and respond to your requests and to provide services you have requested.
  • To Improve Our Services: To better understand your needs and preferences, leading to enhancements in our service delivery.
  • To Communicate: To update you on the status of your requests, send important notices regarding our services, and inform you about changes to our policies.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

4. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you enter, submit, or access your personal information.

5. Third-Party Links

Occasionally, at our discretion, we may include or offer third-party products or services on our website. These third-party sites have separate and independent privacy policies. We therefore have no responsibility or liability for the content and activities of these linked sites.

6. Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes to our practices. We will notify you about significant changes by placing a prominent notice on our website.

7. Contacting Us

If there are any questions regarding this privacy policy, we encourage you to contact us here.

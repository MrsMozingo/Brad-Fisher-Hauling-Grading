Our Equipment

At Brad Fisher Hauling and Grading, we pride ourselves on utilizing a robust fleet of modern and well-maintained equipment to ensure top performance and reliability across all our projects. Here’s a look at the key pieces of equipment we have available to tackle any job, big or small.

Excavators: Our excavators range from mini to full-size to accommodate any job size. These machines are essential for digging, trenching, and demolition, making them versatile for any project needs.

Bulldozers: Known for their power and efficiency, our bulldozers are instrumental in clearing and grading large plots of land. Whether you need to prepare a new construction site or manage a landscape, our bulldozers deliver unmatched capability.

Skid Steer Loaders: Compact yet powerful, our skid steer loaders can maneuver in tight spaces where larger machines cannot. They are perfect for site clean-up, grading, and moving earth with precision.

Backhoe Loaders: Combining the functionality of an excavator and a loader, our backhoes are excellent for medium-scale tasks involving digging, trenching, back-filling, and material handling.

Motor Graders Essential for road construction and maintenance, our motor graders provide precise control for creating flat surfaces. They are also used for setting the foundation in road laying and grading work.

Wheel Loaders Our wheel loaders are crucial for moving large volumes of earth, gravel, sand, and other materials quickly and efficiently. They are ideal for both construction sites and material handling tasks.

Dump Trucks Available for hauling materials to and from the site, our dump trucks ensure that no matter the quantity of material needed or removed, we have the capacity to handle it smoothly and effectively.

Compaction Equipment We have a variety of compaction equipment, including rollers and compactors, which are vital for preparing surfaces to prevent settlement and ensure stability.

Snow Plows and Salt SpreadersEquipped for winter services, our snow plows and salt spreaders are ready to tackle snow removal and ice management, ensuring safety and accessibility during the snowy months.

Snow Clearing During the winter months, we provide reliable snow clearing services to ensure your roads, driveways, and access points are safe and accessible. Our fleet of snow removal equipment is ready to handle even the heaviest snowfalls, offering both scheduled and emergency services.
